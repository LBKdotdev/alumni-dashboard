What This Is — and Isn't
👤Human in the loop. Every outreach reviewed by a coordinator. Nothing sends automatically.
📂Public data only. NPI Registry, PubMed, state boards — federally released or public record.
🚪Opt-out, always. One-click opt-out in every outreach. Alumni who decline are fully removed.
🏛️University-owned. No vendor lock-in. No subscription. Everything belongs to WesternU.

Data Sources
TIER 1 — PUBLIC, AUTOMATED
NPI Registry — monthly, FOIA-released. PubMed — daily. No consent needed.

Doximity, LinkedIn — alumni self-link only. Never a data source.
What Lisa's Team Gets in 30 Days
A working prototype with real COMP data — not a proposal, not a slide deck. Something her team can actually use.
1

Foundation — Detect

Days 1–30
  • NPI enrichment for one graduating class
  • Verified practice locations, specialties, credentials
  • PubMed scan for research-active alumni
  • Working dashboard with real COMP data
  • Clear picture: here's who we can find, here's the gap
2

Signal Engine — Enrich

Days 31–60
  • Automated monitoring: moves, publications, credentials
  • Live signal feed — not sample data
  • Context enrichment against student needs
  • Daily coordinator queue with full context
  • First real outreach recommendations
3

Engagement — Suggest

Days 61–90
  • Mentorship matching: signal-based, specialty-specific
  • Outreach templates by signal type
  • Engagement scoring: who's connecting back
  • COCA reporting: always-current data pulls
  • Two-campus analytics side by side
4

The Loop — Learn

Days 91+
  • Every response feeds back into the system
  • Better timing, better matches
  • Institutional memory that compounds
  • Full lifecycle: student → resident → mentor → advocate
  • The continuum — actually working at scale
The Principle
Each phase earns the next. If Phase 1 doesn't deliver value, you stop — no risk, no sunk cost. The university owns everything. No vendor lock-in. No subscription.
