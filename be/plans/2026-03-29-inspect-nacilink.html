NaciLink — Where We Are

Alumni Relationship Engine for WesternU · Product Owner: Scotty Ledel

What It Is

A tool that puts 6,282 alumni profiles in Lisa Warren's hands — searchable, filterable, with outreach built in. Originally "Alumni Relationship Engine," now branded as NaciLink. Named after Lake Nacimiento where the concept was born. Built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, zero frameworks, zero dependencies beyond Chart.js.
