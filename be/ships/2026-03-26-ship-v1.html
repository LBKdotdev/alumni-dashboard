5 Views, Zero Dependencies

The Alumni Relationship Engine is a fully vanilla SPA — no React, no build tools, no npm. Five views (Dashboard, Directory, Queue, Profile, Projects), Chart.js for the two dashboard charts, and everything else is hand-rolled HTML/CSS/JS.

  • Dashboard — stat cards, specialty bar chart, engagement pie, campus comparison, SOAP story
  • Directory — search, 6 filters, sort, 25/page pagination, 168 alumni cards
  • Queue — trigger-based morning briefing, type-tinted cards, actionable outreach
  • Profile — 5-section deep dive, timeline, notes, publications, outreach history
  • Projects — event planning, alumni targeting, invite tracking, outreach drafting
