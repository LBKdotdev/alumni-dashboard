Ship v2.0 — NaciLink

The tool has a name, a brand, and conference features. This is a product now.

What Shipped

NaciLink Brand Identity

Full rebrand from "Alumni Relationship Engine" to NaciLink. Lake Nacimiento line art in the sidebar. NaciLink emblem on login gate. Custom favicon. Browser tab. PWA manifest — installs as a real app on Lisa's phone home screen.

AACOM Vegas Filter

Teal "AACOM Vegas" pill button in the alumni directory. One tap shows 168 Nevada alumni. Tap again to clear. Lisa opens the directory before the conference and sees everyone near Vegas.

"Met at AACOM" Quick Tag

One-tap button on every profile to tag alumni Lisa meets at the conference. Toggles on and off. Tagged alumni show up in a "Met at AACOM" filter in the directory — her follow-up list in one tap.

Conference Quick Lookup

New "Lookup" view — full-screen search with big input, auto-focused, 20 large result cards. Designed for standing in a crowd. Type a name, tap a result, see the profile.

Alumni-to-Alumni Connections

"Similar Alumni" section on every profile — up to 3 alumni with the same specialty in the same state. Lisa can connect people to each other mid-conversation.

Search Fix — Any Word Order

"Warren Lisa" now finds "Lisa Warren." Works across all search fields — lookup, sidebar, mobile, and directory. Every word just has to appear somewhere in the name, specialty, or city.
